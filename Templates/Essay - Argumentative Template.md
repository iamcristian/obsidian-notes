## Self-Review Checklist
- [ ] Thesis takes a clear, debatable stance
- [ ] Each body paragraph has ONE main argument
- [ ] Evidence is specific and credible
- [ ] Counter-argument is addressed fairly and refuted
- [ ] Logical transitions between paragraphs
- [ ] Conclusion doesn't introduce new arguments
- [ ] Persuasive but not aggressive tone
- [ ] C1 vocabulary throughout

## Useful Argumentative Phrases
| Function | Phrases |
|----------|---------|
| Stating position | "I firmly believe that...", "It is my contention that..." |
| Adding evidence | "This is evidenced by...", "Research demonstrates that..." |
| Emphasizing | "It is crucial to recognize that...", "Significantly,..." |
| Refuting | "This argument fails to consider...", "However, this overlooks..." |
| Concluding | "In light of the evidence...", "Ultimately,..." |
