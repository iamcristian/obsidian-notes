## Self-Review Checklist
- [ ] Hook creates emotional connection immediately
- [ ] Balance of logos (logic), pathos (emotion), ethos (credibility)
- [ ] Reader benefits are clear and compelling
- [ ] Objections are addressed
- [ ] Call to action is specific and actionable
- [ ] Tone is passionate but not manipulative
- [ ] Uses "you" to speak directly to reader
- [ ] Ending is inspiring/memorable

## Persuasive Techniques Used
- [ ] Rhetorical questions
- [ ] Anecdotes/Stories
- [ ] Statistics
- [ ] Expert quotes
- [ ] Emotional language
- [ ] Repetition for emphasis
- [ ] Inclusive language ("we", "our")
- [ ] Future visualization

## Useful Persuasive Phrases
| Function | Phrases |
|----------|---------|
| Connecting | "Have you ever felt...", "We all know that..." |
| Emotional | "Imagine a world where...", "Consider the impact on..." |
| Urgency | "Now is the time to...", "We cannot afford to wait..." |
| Benefits | "You will discover...", "This could transform your..." |
| Call to action | "Take the first step by...", "Join the movement..." |
