## Self-Review Checklist
- [ ] Both subjects are given equal treatment
- [ ] Thesis goes beyond listing similarities/differences
- [ ] Comparison points are parallel (compare same aspects)
- [ ] Structure (block or point-by-point) is consistent
- [ ] Analysis explains WHY comparisons matter
- [ ] Transitions clearly signal comparison/contrast
- [ ] Conclusion synthesizes the comparison meaningfully
